WHY TWO PROGRAMS?

Football and cheer are two sides of the same game day experience. Football athletes develop competitive skills through combine testing and 7 on 7 play. Cheerleaders develop performance skills and stage presence through sideline routines and game day energy. Every Friday, both camps come together at our turf stadium where football competes and cheer performs. It's the real Friday night lights experience that builds champions on and off the field.

GAME DAY FRIDAYS

Every Friday, camp moves to our turf stadium for the most exciting day of the week.

Football athletes compete in 7 on 7 scrimmages, applying the week's training in live game situations. No full contact, just speed, strategy, and skill.

Cheerleaders perform their sideline routines, supporting the teams, bringing the energy, and building confidence in front of the crowd.

Each week builds toward Championship Day in Week 6, where families tailgate, music plays, and athletes showcase six weeks of growth in the ultimate game day celebration. Winning football teams receive championship trophies. Cheer squads perform their complete 2.5 minute routine. It's the finale that brings it all together.

BEYOND SUMMER: MUMMERS PARADE

Summer camp is just the beginning. The WCFCC family stays connected through October as we prepare for the Alsatia Mummers Parade, Hagerstown's largest annual event with over 70,000 spectators.

HALLOWEEN NIGHT | OCTOBER 31, 2026

WCFCC takes the streets of downtown Hagerstown with our own custom built float, cheer performances, and full team representation.

CHEERLEADERS

Perform choreographed routines on our float and walk the parade route bringing energy and school spirit to thousands of spectators.

FOOTBALL ATHLETES

Demonstrate athletic drills and football fundamentals while walking the parade route. Post camp practices provide ongoing skills training and coaching to keep you sharp for your fall teams.

THE FLOAT BUILD

Athletes and families work together to design and build our parade float. This is a team effort that brings everyone closer and creates memories that last beyond the field.

Post Camp Schedule: Meet 3x per month (August, September, October) to practice routines, run drills, and work on the float until parade day.

All parade activities included in camp registration. No additional fees.
