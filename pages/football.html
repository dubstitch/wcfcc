COMBINE
What Is A

COMBINE
EXPLAINED

A combine is a structured athletic evaluation designed to measure and develop key performance metrics through standardized drills and testing. Athletes participate in drills that test speed, agility, strength, and position specific skills.

COMBINE DRILLS

◆40 Yard Dash: Measures straight line speed
◆20 Yard Shuttle: Tests lateral quickness and change of direction
◆3 Cone Drill: Evaluates agility and body control
◆Broad Jump: Measures explosive lower body power
◆Vertical Jump: Tests vertical explosion
◆Position Specific Drills: Evaluates technique for each position

Safety First

OUR
STANDARDS

USA Football Heads Up Certification

All coaches are certified in USA Football's Heads Up program, emphasizing proper tackling technique and player safety.

Safe Contact Protocols

Age appropriate contact progressions ensure athletes learn fundamentals safely before advancing to full contact.

Equipment and Safety

All equipment is properly fitted and regularly inspected. Athletes are taught how to wear and maintain their gear.

Skill Development First

Technique and fundamentals are prioritized over physicality. We build smart, skilled players who understand the game.
