Camp Logistics

FOOD,
FACILITIES, GEAR

Food & Hydration

Eat before arriving. Water and Gatorade provided. Light snacks at every practice. Snooks Shaved Ice & Soft Serve caters one weekday + every Friday. Friday game days include a full meal.

Facilities

No bathrooms on-site during weekday practices. Use the restroom before arriving. Friday games at the Turf Stadium have full facilities.

Gear Responsibility

The 7 on 7 helmet must come to every practice and game day. Camp gear is WCFCC property — lost or damaged equipment is replaced at parent cost.

Bring Daily

Sunscreen, water bottle (optional second one for hot days), and a positive attitude. The little things matter.
