POST CAMP SCHEDULE

AUGUST THROUGH OCTOBER

Athletes meet 3 times per month for skills training, coaching support, and Mummers Parade float building.

• Focus: Ongoing skills development, drill work, parade choreography
• Duration: Approximately 2 hours per session
• Parade Day: October 31, 2026 (Halloween)

IMPORTANT INFORMATION

Arrival Time

Athletes should arrive 15 minutes early to get settled and ready.

Weather Cancellations

Families will be notified via email and text if practice is canceled due to severe weather.

What to Bring

7 on 7 helmet (every practice), water bottle, sunscreen, cleats (optional but recommended).
