Heads Up

GOOD TO
KNOW

Arrive Early

Plan to arrive about 15 minutes before practice. Coaches are on-site 30 minutes before. Pickup is prompt — please be on time.

Weather Policy

We operate rain or shine. If we cancel, you’ll be notified at least 2 hours before practice via email and text alert.

What to Bring

Water bottle, sunscreen, athletic clothes. Football also brings 7 on 7 helmet and (optional) cleats. Cheer brings athletic shoes and hair ties.

Attendance Rule

Athletes who miss more than 3 practices are not eligible for Championship Day games. Communicate absences to the coaches.
