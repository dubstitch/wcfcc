Required Documents

FORMS
CHECKLIST

Five forms total. The registration form goes in at signup. The other four must be turned in before the first practice.

1. Registration Form

Due: At signup

Athlete info, parent/guardian contact, program selection.

2. Birth Certificate

Due: Before first practice

Required to verify age division placement.

3. Medical Release

Due: Before first practice

Allergies, conditions, emergency contacts, treatment authorization.

4. Code of Conduct

Due: Before first practice

Athlete + parent expectations and signatures.

5. Photo / Video Release

Due: Before first practice

Permission for camp photos and video used in promotion.

Policies

THE
FINE PRINT

Fee

$280 per athlete. Covers uniform, all six weeks of training, Friday game days, Championship Day, post-camp practices, and Mummers Parade.

Deadline

Registration closes April 30, 2026. Spots are limited and divisions fill on a first-come basis.

Payment Methods

Credit card, debit card, or cash. Payment plans available on request — just ask.

Refund Policy

100% refund up to 2 weeks before camp starts. 50% up to 1 week before. After camp begins, no refund except medical withdrawals (prorated, director discretion).

Attendance

Athletes who miss more than 3 practices are not eligible to participate in Championship Day games.

Equipment

All camp gear is WCFCC property. Athletes are responsible for keeping it. Lost or damaged gear is replaced at parent cost.
