Required documents and important policies for all WCFCC athletes.

CAMP POLICIES

ATTENDANCE POLICY

Regular attendance is expected for all scheduled practices and game days. Athletes who miss more than 3 practices may not be eligible to participate in Championship Day.

Excused absences: Illness, family emergency, or prior commitment communicated in advance.

Notification: Parents must notify coaches at least 24 hours in advance when possible.

REFUND POLICY

Full refund (100%): Up to 2 weeks before camp starts

Partial refund (50%): Up to 1 week before camp starts

No refund: After camp begins

Medical withdrawals with doctor's note may be eligible for prorated refund at director's discretion.

DISCIPLINARY POLICY

Athletes are expected to respect coaches, teammates, and camp rules at all times.

First offense: Verbal warning and parent notification

Second offense: Suspension from one practice

Third offense: Removal from camp with no refund

Serious violations (fighting, bullying, endangering others) may result in immediate removal.

WEATHER POLICY

Camp operates rain or shine unless severe weather requires cancellation.

Cancellation notification: Email and text sent at least 2 hours before practice

Makeup sessions: Canceled practices will be rescheduled when possible

Extreme heat: Additional water breaks and shortened practice duration as needed

EQUIPMENT POLICY

All camp-provided equipment (uniforms, helmets, pom-poms) remains the property of WCFCC and must be returned at the end of camp.

Lost or damaged equipment: Replacement cost billed to parent/guardian

Personal equipment: Athletes responsible for their own cleats, water bottles, and personal items

COMMUNICATION POLICY

Primary communication will be via email and text message to the contact information provided at registration.

Parent questions: Contact coaches before or after practice, or email WCFootballcombinecamp@gmail.com

Emergency contact: Kahlil Johnson 240-591-8453 or Des Wade 301-332-3441

INJURY POLICY

All coaches are CPR and First Aid certified. Minor injuries will be treated on site with basic first aid.

Serious injuries: Parents will be contacted immediately and 911 called if necessary

Return from injury: Doctor's clearance required for injuries requiring medical attention

Insurance: Parents are responsible for all medical costs. WCFCC does not provide medical insurance.
