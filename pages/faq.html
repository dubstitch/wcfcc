Quick answers to common questions about WCFCC Summer Camp.

CAMP DETAILS

Camp runs from June 15 to July 24, 2026. That's 6 weeks of training. Post camp activities (3x per month) continue through October for Mummers Parade preparation.

Football: Monday & Wednesday, 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM. Friday Game Days: 6:00 PM to 8:30 PM.

Cheer: Tuesday & Thursday, 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM. Friday Game Days: 6:00 PM to 8:30 PM.

Football: Ages 9 to 17 (Three divisions: Boost 9-11, Nitro 12-14, Turbo 15-17)

Cheer: Ages 8 to 11 (One squad: Glow)

Practice locations are provided after registration. Friday Game Days are held at our turf stadium in Hagerstown.

No experience required! We welcome all skill levels. Coaches teach proper fundamentals from the ground up and work with athletes at their own pace.

Complete uniform package (shirt, shorts, helmet/pom-poms, accessories), all meals and snacks during camp, Championship Day experience, post camp practices, and Mummers Parade participation. Everything except personal items like cleats and water bottles.

SAFETY & POLICIES

Yes. All coaches are background checked, CPR and First Aid certified. Football coaches hold USA Football Heads Up certification. Cheer coaches hold AACCA Spirit Safety certification.

Minor injuries are treated on site with basic first aid. For serious injuries, parents are contacted immediately and emergency services called if needed. Doctor's clearance required before returning from injury.

Camp operates rain or shine unless severe weather requires cancellation. Families will be notified via email and text at least 2 hours before practice. Canceled practices are rescheduled when possible.

LOGISTICS

Practice days: Comfortable athletic clothes, water bottle, sunscreen. Football athletes bring 7 on 7 helmet.

Game days: Camp uniform (provided), helmet/pom-poms, cleats (optional), sunscreen.

Light snacks and hydration provided during weekday practices. Full meals provided on Friday Game Days. Athletes should eat before arriving at camp.

No bathrooms on site during weekday practices. Athletes should use the restroom before arriving. Bathrooms available at the turf stadium on Fridays.
