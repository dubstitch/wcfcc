Safety & Policies

SAFETY
FIRST

Yes. Football coaches are USA Football Heads Up certified. Cheer coaches are AACCA Spirit Safety certified. All staff are background-checked and current in CPR & First Aid.
Minor injuries get basic first aid on-site. For anything serious, we contact parents immediately and call 911 if needed. Insurance is the parent’s responsibility.
We operate rain or shine. Cancellations are decided at least 2 hours before practice and communicated by email and text alert. In extreme heat we add water breaks and shade time.
Athletes who miss more than 3 practices are not eligible to play in Championship Day games. Always communicate absences to the coaches in advance.
Standard progression: verbal redirection, then suspension, then removal in serious cases. Code of Conduct is signed at registration and applies to athletes and parents.

Logistics

GAME DAY
DETAILS

Water bottle, sunscreen, athletic clothes, positive attitude. Football also brings the 7 on 7 helmet (every day) and optional cleats. Cheer brings athletic shoes and hair ties.
Athletes should eat before arriving. We provide water and Gatorade plus light snacks. Snooks Shaved Ice & Soft Serve caters one weekday and every Friday. Friday game days include a full meal.
No bathrooms during weekday practices — please use the restroom before arriving. The Turf Stadium does have facilities for Friday game days.
