ARRIVAL & DROP OFF

  • • Athletes should arrive 15 minutes early to get settled and ready
  • • Coaches will be on site 30 minutes before practice begins
  • • Athletes must be picked up promptly at the end of practice
  • • Late pickups require advance notice to coaches

WHAT TO BRING EVERY DAY

  • ✓ Water bottle (water and Gatorade provided, but bring your own for easy access)
  • ✓ Sunscreen
  • ✓ Athletic shoes (sneakers or cheer shoes)
  • ✓ Hair ties and bobby pins
  • ✓ Positive attitude and ready to work

FOOD & HYDRATION

Before Camp:

Athletes should eat a full meal before arriving. Camp begins with high energy conditioning and skill work, so proper nutrition is essential.

During Camp:

  • • Water and Gatorade provided throughout practice
  • • Light snacks provided (fruit, etc.)
  • • Snooks Shaved Ice & Soft Serve caters one weekday practice and every Friday Game Day

Game Day Fridays:

Full meals provided for all athletes and families are welcome to enjoy the tailgate atmosphere.

FACILITIES

No bathrooms on site. Athletes should use the restroom before arriving at camp.

Shade and rest areas are available. Athletes are encouraged to stay hydrated and take breaks as needed.

WHAT TO WEAR

Practice Days (Tuesday & Thursday):

Comfortable athletic clothes appropriate for outdoor training. Athletic shorts and t-shirt. Hair pulled back securely. No camp uniform required.

Game Days (Fridays):

  • ★ Camp uniform (t-shirt and shorts)
  • ★ Pom-poms
  • ★ Hair accessories (bow or scrunchie)
  • ★ Athletic shoes

This is the day to represent WCFCC with pride and team spirit.

WEATHER POLICY

Camp operates rain or shine unless severe weather conditions require cancellation. Families will be notified via email and text if practice is canceled due to weather.

In case of extreme heat:

Additional water breaks and shade time will be provided. Athletes should bring extra water and wear sunscreen.

ILLNESS & INJURY

  • • If your athlete is sick, please keep them home to protect the health of all campers
  • • Minor injuries will be treated by coaching staff with basic first aid
  • • In case of serious injury, parents will be contacted immediately
  • • All coaches are trained in CPR and basic sports safety protocols

PARENT INVOLVEMENT

Practice Days:

Parents are welcome to watch from designated viewing areas but are not required to stay.

Game Day Fridays:

Families are encouraged to attend, tailgate, and cheer on the athletes. Bring chairs, tents, and food to enjoy the full game day experience.

Championship Day (Week 6):

This is the BIG day. All families are invited to celebrate six weeks of growth, performance, and community. Team photos and awards ceremony follow the championship performances.

COMMUNICATION

How We'll Keep You Updated:

  • • Weekly email updates with schedule changes and reminders
  • • Text notifications for urgent updates or weather cancellations
  • • Coaches available before and after practice for questions

Questions or Concerns?

Contact us at WCFootballcombinecamp@gmail.com or speak directly with a coach at pickup/drop off.

CODE OF CONDUCT

We expect all athletes, families, and coaches to uphold the values of respect, discipline, and sportsmanship.

Athletes are expected to:

  • • Arrive on time and ready to participate
  • • Respect coaches, teammates, and opponents
  • • Give full effort in every drill and activity
  • • Support each other and build team unity

Parents are expected to:

  • • Support positive coaching and avoid sideline coaching
  • • Communicate respectfully with staff and other families
  • • Model good sportsmanship for young athletes
