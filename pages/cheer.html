About the Program

CAMP
PHILOSOPHY

CONFIDENCE & TEAMWORK

A 6-week sideline cheer program designed to build confident young women through technique, teamwork, and real game-day performance experience.

SKILL & SHOWMANSHIP

Athletes learn cheers, motions, jumps, and choreography while developing confidence, leadership, and a genuine sense of team pride.

SIDELINE
What You Learn

CHEER
CURRICULUM

Weekly practices build technical skill, stamina, and performance confidence. Every rep is focused on being ready for Friday's live sideline reps at the turf stadium.

CORE SKILLS

◆Sideline Cheers — Crowd-leading chants and call-and-response.
◆Motions & Jumps — Sharp technique and clean execution.
◆Choreography — Full sideline dances set to music.
◆Showmanship — Stage presence, energy, and crowd connection.
◆Conditioning — Strength, stamina, and flexibility.
◆Teamwork — Synchronization and communication.

Safety First

OUR
STANDARDS

Certified Cheer Staff

All coaches are background-checked and trained in youth cheer safety, proper progressions, and spotting technique.

Age-Appropriate Progressions

Skills are introduced in a safe sequence. Athletes master fundamentals before advancing to complex choreography.

Inclusive Environment

All experience levels welcome. We meet every athlete where she is and build from there.

Confidence Over Perfection

We build confident, coachable performers. Technique matters, but so does the courage to perform in front of a crowd.

The Journey

SIX WEEK
PROGRESSION

1

FOUNDATIONS

Cheer voice, motion placement, jump prep, first sideline cheers, and intro to choreography.

2

TECHNIQUE BUILD

Jump technique, motion sharpness, and adding the first half of the Championship Day routine.

3

STAMINA & ENERGY

Conditioning for performance stamina, crowd engagement, and synchronized team execution.

4

FULL ROUTINE

Complete 2.5 minute routine learned. Polish formations, transitions, and timing.

5

PERFORMANCE POLISH

Performance-quality reps, sharpening showmanship, locking in sideline cheer rotation for Friday.

6

CHAMPIONSHIP DAY

Perform the full 2.5 minute routine during the Championship Day games. Families, stadium energy, pure showcase.
