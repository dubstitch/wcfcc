The Journey

CHEER
CURRICULUM

Every rep is built around the next Friday performance. Six weeks of progressive skill, choreography, and showmanship — ending with a full 2.5-minute Championship Day routine.

1

FOUNDATIONS

Cheer voice, motion placement, jump prep, first sideline cheers.

2

TECHNIQUE BUILD

Jump technique, motion sharpness, first half of the routine.

3

STAMINA & ENERGY

Conditioning, crowd engagement, synchronized execution.

4

FULL ROUTINE

Complete 2.5-minute routine learned. Polish formations and timing.

5

POLISH

Performance reps. Showmanship. Friday rotation locked in.

6

CHAMPIONSHIP DAY

Full routine performed live. Families, stadium, showcase energy.
