Core Values

WHAT MAKES US
DIFFERENT

We don't just train athletes. We build character, community, and confidence through every rep, every Friday, and every season.

ELITE TRAINING
Combine style evaluation with position specific coaching from experienced staff who genuinely invest in every athlete's growth week over week.
GAME DAY FRIDAYS
Every Friday is game day at our turf stadium. Football runs 7 on 7 scrimmages while cheer performs sideline routines. Real game reps every single week.
CHAMPIONSHIP DAY
Week 6 is Championship Day. Football players compete for the title. Cheer squads perform their final showcase. Trophies, families, and full stadium energy.
ALL INCLUSIVE
$175. That's it. No hidden fees. Your uniform, your gear, your Mummers Parade experience. Everything included so families can focus on the fun.
SAFE COACHING
All coaches are background checked and trained in youth sports safety protocols. The safety of every athlete is not negotiable.
CLEAR COMMUNICATION
Families stay informed with regular updates and transparent policies. No surprises. Just straight talk and consistent follow through.
COMMUNITY FOCUS
More than a team, it's a family. WCFCC is rooted in Hagerstown and built to invest back into the community that shows up for us.
